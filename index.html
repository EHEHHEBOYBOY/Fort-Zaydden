WHO WE ARE

About the MED

The Military Education Department (MED) is the foremost authority on rules enforcement and military conduct within Fort Zaydden. Our mission is to uphold the highest standards of discipline, professionalism, and tactical excellence across all branches of service.

Founded to ensure every soldier understands their duties and obligations, the MED operates under the direct supervision of High Command (HICOM) and is responsible for training, evaluating, and certifying personnel throughout the garrison.

Whether you are a new recruit seeking orientation or a seasoned officer looking to refine your skills, the MED provides the resources, guidance, and structure necessary to excel in the field.

Requirements

To join the Military Education Department, all applicants must meet the following minimum requirements. These standards ensure the integrity and effectiveness of our operations at Fort Zaydden.

✓
Active Game Account Must hold an active account in the Fort Zaydden game environment.
✓
Minimum Rank: Private Applicants must have achieved at least the rank of Private before applying.
✓
Clean Disciplinary Record No outstanding violations or disciplinary actions within the past 30 days.
✓
Teamspeak / Discord Access Must be able to communicate via the official Fort Zaydden communication channels.
✓
Weekly Activity Commitment Minimum of 5 hours per week of active participation in operations.
✓
Completed Basic Training Successful completion of the Fort Zaydden Basic Training program is mandatory.

Tryouts

How to Apply

Tryouts for the Military Education Department are held on a rolling basis. Qualified candidates will be contacted within 48 hours of submitting their application. The tryout process consists of three stages:

01
Application Review Your background and record are reviewed by MED officers.
02
Field Assessment A live in-game evaluation of your skills and conduct.
03
Interview & Induction A brief interview with HICOM followed by formal induction.
